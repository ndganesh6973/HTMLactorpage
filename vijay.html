vijay

Vijay is an Indian actor and playback singer who works in Tamil cinema. He has won numerous awards, including three Tamil Nadu State Film Awards and seven Vijay Awards. He is one of the highest-paid actors in Tamil cinema and has a huge fan following. Vijay made his acting debut in 1992 with the film "Naalaiya Theerpu" and has since appeared in over 60 films. Some of his notable films include "Ghilli," "Thuppakki," "Mersal," and "Bigil." He is known for his charismatic screen presence, versatile acting skills, and energetic dance performances. Vijay is also involved in philanthropic activities and has a strong social media presence, connecting with his fans worldwide.
